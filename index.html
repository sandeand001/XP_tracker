Chapter IThe Daily Ritual

A simple loop — less than five minutes.

1
Teach as Normal — No extra prep. Just run your class.
2
Tick the Checklist — Open Daily Checklist. Mark quiz, participation, kindness.
3
Log Any Behavior — Issues? Record on Behavior. None? Skip it.
4
Process XP ✨ — Hit Process Daily XP on the Tracker. Done.

Chapter IIIGame Mechanics

The arcane systems behind the scenes.

⚡ XP Sources

Quiz +10 · Expectations +10 · Faculty +5 · Exit Ticket +5 · Writing +5 · Kindness +5

Plus 5 participation mini-checks (+2 each), custom bonuses, and test-score mapping.

🛡️ Guilds

⚔ Blue · ⚔ Gold · ⚔ Purple — Teams for friendly competition or cooperative goals.

🔥 Streaks

🔥 5 days · 💥 10 days · 💎 15 days · 🏆 20 days
≥3 participation checks per day keeps the streak alive.

⚖️ The Debt System

+XP Earned → Pays Debt → Levels Up
Debt is temporary. Future XP pays it off first, then advances.
